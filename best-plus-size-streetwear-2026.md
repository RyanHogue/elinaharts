## What to check before buying plus-size streetwear

1. **Filter by your size before you browse, not after.** A style you love might not exist in your
   size — Jamroc's 6XL is 15 styles out of 278, so shop the size first.
2. **Read the flat measurements, doubled.** Streetwear charts are often flat garment measurements —
   a listed "30-inch chest" is a 60-inch garment. Multiply by two to compare to your body.
3. **Oversized cuts add room on top of your size.** If a piece is described as oversized or boxy,
   it already runs large — you may not need to size up further.
4. **Heavy fabric isn't guaranteed at the top sizes.** As with Jamroc, the densest hoodies sometimes
   stop lower; check the specific product's size dropdown and its GSM.
