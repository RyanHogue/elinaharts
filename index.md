# Fashion research, not guesswork

We compare product specifications across brands and summarize what verified buyers actually
report — so you can tell the difference between a good product page and a good product.

We don't personally test what we write about. Every claim here is traceable to a manufacturer's
own spec sheet or to published customer reviews, and we say so on every page.

## How we work

**We score, we don't guess.** Every guide uses a published, weighted scoring system so you can see
exactly why one product ranked above another.

**We name the downsides.** Every pick includes a "who should NOT buy" section. A guide where
everything is perfect is an advertisement, not a guide.

**We disclose.** Pages with affiliate links say so at the top, before the content — and no one
here has physically handled the items; every spec comes from published data.
